About Work Travel

Ali Richomme

I was born in Jersey, Channel Islands and I grew up loving the sciences, music and art. Since leaving the island in 2012 to go to the University of Bristol to study Engineering Mathematics, I started programming and excelled in Data Modelling and Analytics. Upon graduation with a first class honours masters degree, I started work as a Data Scientist at a small Bristol-based company called Polecat Intelligence who primarily work with the risk and reputation of corporations. After progressing through the ranks to Senior Data Scientist, I left Polecat in 2019 to pursue my other passion; Travel.

Setting out on my first long-term solo-trip, I travelled from Mexico all the way through Latin America to Argentina. Towards the end of my time in South America, I begun volunteering and helped run a hostel in Huanchaco, Peru. COVID-19 saw an abrupt end to my travelling and I helped to set-up and run a charity which provided food to families in need during quarantine in Huanchaco.

I returned to Jersey in June 2020 and began working for Altair Partners as an IT consultant.
